Attack, Dash, Disengage, Dodge, Grapple, Help, Hide, Improvise, Influence, Magic, Ready, Search, Shove, Study, Utilize
Grapple: You can use your action to grapple an opponent. You need to make a Prowess combat roll. If you succeed you hold the opponent grappled. A grappled person has disadvantage on their attack rolls and others have advantage when attacking them.
Shove: You can use your action to shove an opponent. You need to make a Dexterity combat roll. If you succeed you push the opponent 5ft away and knock them prone.
Mounting and dismounting: You can use your action to get on or get off your steed of choice.

Off-Hand Attack: When you take the Attack action on your turn and attack with a Light weapon, you can make one extra attack as a Bonus Action later on the same turn. That extra attack must be made with a different Light weapon, and you don't add your ability modifier to the extra attack's damage unless that modifier is negative or you have an ability that tells you otherwise.
Swapping Weapons: You can use your Bonus Action to make a full swap of weapons. Unless you are duel wielding weapons then it will take a full Action.
Interacting with the Environment: If you want to interact with your surroundings; like closing a door, breaking a branch, flipping a table, it will cost you a Bonus Action.
